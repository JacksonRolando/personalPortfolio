Wisconsin Decarceration Platform
The Wisconsin Decarceration Platform is a group based in Milwaukee formed to provide resources to those impacted by the criminal justice system. I worked very closely with people with less experience with technology to turn their ideas into features of the site. I was able to communicate my technical ideas in ways they could understand and contribute to. I took information I was presented with, moved it, and formatted it in a way that is easier to digest and sift through so that visitors to the site can find what they are looking for more quickly. I used Weebly's many available components in addition to some I made myself in order to overcome Weebly's shortcomings to present information effectively.
